Welcome Guest

Welcome to Pin and Throw, a community-based digital waste-reporting system designed to bridge the communication gap between local residents and officials. In many urban vicinities, unmanaged garbage often lingers on sidewalks, creating "indecent areas" that pose environmental health risks and lead to clogged drainage systems. Our platform empowers residents to take an active role in maintaining their community's cleanliness by pinning the exact locations of waste hotspots on an interactive map.

To ensure high accountability and prevent fraudulent submissions, Pin and Throw requires users to upload photographic evidence for every report. This real-time information is immediately transmitted to a centralized dashboard for barangay officers, allowing them to assess, verify, and track the progress of cleanup operations. By streamlining communication, our system ensures that waste is addressed rapidly, helping to mitigate flood risks and promote overall urban organization.

This initiative is deeply rooted in supporting the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals, specifically Goal 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities and Goal 16: Peace, Justice, and Strong Institutions. Initially focused on addressing challenges within Barangay Pio Del Pilar, Pin and Throw seeks to create a cleaner, safer, and more organized environment for all.
